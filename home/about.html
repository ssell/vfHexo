Brief

My name is Steven Sell and I am a software engineer living in Orlando, FL. I have over six years professional experience in the Modeling & Simulation industry with special interest in Graphics, Engine, and Tools programming. Though preferring C++, I have multiple years of experience in using Java and C# as well.

VertexFragment is my personal website, which is used primarily as a landing spot for recent projects, though one day I hope to write articles and guides covering advanced rendering techniques. But seeing that there is a limited number of hours in the day, my spare time is usually devoted to working on my pet engine, Ocular.
